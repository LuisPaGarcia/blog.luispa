Last weekend, I went to the beach to disconnect, slow down, and reflect on what I want for this next chapter.

I spent the whole day there with my girlfriend and my dog. I ran 5K along the shoreline, felt the sun, listened to the waves, and allowed myself to just be present. 

No noise. No urgency. Just space to think.

**There’s something powerful about stepping away from the routine to gain perspective.** When you create the right environment, the right questions start to surface — and little by little, so do the answers.

I’ve realized how important it is to find the place where you can sit with uncertainty and make peace with it. The place where you ask yourself the questions you’ve been postponing — and actually listen.

For me, that place is the beach.

There’s beauty in completion. And always faith in the unknown.
